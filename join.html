welcome to join us




Our research group is looking for a long-term postdoctoral and research assistant. Welcome to join Zhou-Lab.





Job Wanted 1:Postdoctoral




Recruitment Requirements:


We are looking for postdoc positions in genetics, genomics, population genetics, horticulture, and computational biology.

We will conduct molecular biology experiments and bioinformatics analysis, jointly analyze genome and epigenome data, provide theoretical basis for in-depth analysis of the genetic basis of key agronomic traits, develop relevant analysis methods, and carry out grape breeding research.




To apply for conditions:


1. Love scientific research, have ideal, and willing to struggle for the ideal down to earth.

2. Good reading and writing skills in Both Chinese and English. Able to write English papers independently, able to track professional literature and master international cutting-edge research trends in related fields.

3. Good verbal and written communication skills, as well as the skills necessary to participate and organize team work.

4. Capable of managing and supervising the completion of research projects, and able to initiate and design new research projects according to the research progress.

5. PhD degree in genomics, bioinformatics, genetics, botany, crop genetics and Breeding, etc. PhD students in other disciplines may also be contacted if they have a strong interest.




Post treatment:


1. According to the relevant regulations of Shenzhen City and Dapeng New Area, postdoctoral fellows can enjoy the living allowance for postdoctoral fellows during their stay at the station. The specific amount is subject to the latest regulations of the government, which is 600,000 yuan after tax at present (distributed in two years).

2. Provide post-doctoral salary, five social insurances and housing fund and performance benefits according to relevant regulations of the institute.

3. Postdoctoral fellows can choose to settle down in Shenzhen, and their spouses and minor children can go through household relocation, and enjoy the living allowance for newly introduced talents in Shenzhen and Dapeng New Area. The specific amount is subject to the latest regulations of the government, and the total amount is 60,000 yuan after tax at present (distributed in two years).

4. Postdocs who stay in Shenzhen and meet the requirements can be identified as high-level reserve talents of Shenzhen And receive talent awards. The recognition standards and award amounts shall be subject to the latest regulations of the government.






Job Wanted 2:Research assistant




Recruitment Requirements:


We are looking for a research assistant in biology field.

Assist PI in preparing academic materials; Assist fund project application and management; Complete other scientific research related work assigned.




To apply for conditions:


1. Bachelor degree or above, major including but not limited to bioinformatics, computer science, statistics, mathematics, biology.

2. Some programming ability is preferred.

3. Interested in agriculture and horticulture.

4. Serious and responsible in work, strong sense of time, positive attitude, good communication skills and team spirit.




Post treatment:


1. Pre-tax monthly salary is 8000-12000 RMB, negotiable according to ability and experience.

2. Provide solid scientific research training and assist in recommending students to well-known scientific research institutes at home and abroad for further study.

3. Encourage the publication of academic papers as the first or co-author, and provide promotion channels for scientific research.

4. Social insurance provident fund and other benefits in line with the talent policy shall be subject to the latest relevant regulations of Shenzhen.

5. Based on the concept of balancing scientific research and life, we will have flexible arrangements in terms of vacation and work.






Application way:


Please send your resume (including educational experience, research experience, published papers), representative work and research intention to zhouyongfeng@caas.cn. Please mark "name + position name" in the subject line and compressed file of the email.


The institute will carefully analyze and keep confidential all application information, and contact the successful candidate by phone or E-mail within one month of receiving the application to arrange an interview. Those who fail to pass the qualification examination will not be notified otherwise.
